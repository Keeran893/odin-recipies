ICED MOCHA FUSION SHAKE

DESCRIPTION:

A perfect Combo of Ice, Milk, and Beans to make you day!

Do not try it if you don't go with good things! Cause its AWESOME.

INGREDIENTS:

  • Â¾ cup milk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 3 tablespoons granulated sugar
  • 3 tablespoons mocha flavored instant coffee mix
  • 1 cup crushed ice
  • Optional: Ice-cream

FEW STEPS TO GET IT DONE:

  1. In a blender or food processor, combine milk, vanilla, sugar, coffee powder and crushed ice.
  2. Blend until smooth.
  3. Pour into glasses and serve.
  4. Add ice-cream on the top!
